# Terms

Swopy uses three main terms throughout

- **Number**: An abstract mathematical entity used to describe the quantity of something.

- **Numeral**: The representation of the number in the numeral system, e.g. as a `int`, `float` or a `str`.

- **Denotation**: The number that the system is representing, an `int`, a `float`, a `Fraction` etc. 

## Excuse me?

The numerals `5`, `I`, `𑁖`, and `𐤛𐤚` are all represent the same quantity - the number 5. `5` happens to be the symbol that denotes 5 in Western Arabic numerals (the others numerals are Roman, Brahmi and Phoenician).

The numeral `I` (or `5`) is the signifier - the pixels.
The number 5 is the signified, the mathematical concept being referred to.
The denotation is the relationship between the two.

The number 5 is the denotation of the numerals `5`,` I`, `𑁖`, and `𐤛𐤚`.

Couldn't they all be numbers? Yes, but that would be even more confusing.
